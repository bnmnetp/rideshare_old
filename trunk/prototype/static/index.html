Where do you want to go?

To add a Ride, click on a destination or starting point that is not Decorah. Then, choose whether the point is a starting point, destination, or a commute to Decorah, enter the name for this location (for other ridesharers to see) and click 'OK'.

You can also add yourself to a ride by clicking on a ride on the map and selecting 'Join this ride'.

However, you can't join a ride the day before the trip leaves.
